# Social Media Marketing – OneOhOne Jewelry

**Assignment | Noroff Digital Marketing | March 2026**  
**Brand:** OneOhOne Jewelry &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Platforms:** TikTok & Instagram

---

## Overview

This project documents the development and execution of a social media marketing strategy for OneOhOne Jewelry — a real, active brand. It includes brand identity guidelines, platform strategy, live content creation, and post-performance analysis using real analytics data from TikTok Studio and Instagram Insights.

This is not a simulated exercise. The content was published, and the data is real.

## Key Skills Demonstrated

- **Brand Identity** — Defined personality, tone of voice, colour palette, typography, and visual style
- **Platform Strategy** — Distinct strategies for TikTok (discovery/reach) and Instagram (brand-building)
- **Content Creation** — 4 live posts: 2 TikTok (carousel + video), 2 Instagram (carousel + reel)
- **Performance Analysis** — Real metrics: views, reach, retention, audience demographics, completion rates
- **Engagement Strategy** — Hook testing, caption structure, hashtag strategy, CTA placement
- **Reflection & Iteration** — Identified weaknesses and proposed data-driven improvements

---

## Highlights from the Data

**TikTok Video (Confidence & lifestyle):**
- 668 views in first 24 hours
- 100% new viewers, 100% non-followers
- 97.7% reached via For You page
- Audience: 91% female, 18–34 | Top locations: Norway, Sweden, Denmark

**TikTok Carousel (Educational):**
- 83% new viewers, 81% non-followers
- 4 comments — strong early engagement signal for a new account

**Instagram Reel (Product – Alex bracelet):**
- Average watch time (15s) exceeded video length (8s) — confirms replay behaviour
